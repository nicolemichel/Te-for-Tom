SEIZE

Seize is a track about making the most of each and every day, something I, and many others, have struggled to do, lockdown after lockdown. The song touches on themes of moving forward, getting in touch with our inner power, The song touches on themes of moving forward, getting in touch with our inner power, and being hardened from the past. The perspective portrayed in this song is that nothing is able to affect us unless we let it and that the past has taken much more of a toll on us than it should have. However, we've learned from our mistakes and are moving on. We now see that the sky's the limit and that we will push past this limit, since we are now living each day like it is our last. We don't know what the future has in store, but we can give ourselves the security that it will be worth the journey by doing everything we can daily to set ourselves up for success. This is a theme song for those of us who are constantly striving to get in touch with our best selves, regardless of what anyone else has to say, or what struggles we meet along the way.
