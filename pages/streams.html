During the pandemic, many of us were lost for how to spend our time and I was no different. I was looking for connections and a way to still showcase my music to the world, this is where livestreaming entered my life. Through something I had originally thought was just for video games, I forged lifelong connections with other artists around the world and was able to find an outlet to share not just my music but my personality on a global scale. Some of my best memories from the past couple years I have livestreaming to thank for and even though the world is now at a point where live music exists again, my streams will still be an integral part of my journey as a musician! If you are not able to see me perform in person then I cordially invite you to do the next best thing and catch me on Twitch!
