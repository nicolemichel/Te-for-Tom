Being adopted always ingrained in me the idea that I never really belonged. Music was my only cure for this feeling so I started writing songs at the age of four years old and demos going back as early as eight years old.

It took a while for me to figure out that this was more than just a pastime for me but since the first time I stepped in front of an audience to perform, there was no going back. It became everything from my coping mechanism to my life-source.
